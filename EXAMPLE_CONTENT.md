## üåü PORTFOLIO HIGHLIGHT TIPS

### For Your Best Projects, Include:
1. **Clear Description** - What problem did you solve?
2. **Technologies Used** - What tools/languages did you use?
3. **Results/Impact** - What was the outcome? Numbers help!
4. **What You Learned** - Key takeaways from the project
5. **Live Demo or Link** - Let people experience your work

### Impact Metrics That Impress:
- Sales increase: "Increased conversion by 25%"
- Time saved: "Reduced processing time by 40%"
- User growth: "Grew user base from 1K to 10K users"
- Performance: "Improved page load time by 60%"
- Efficiency: "Automated process saving 20 hours/week"
- Engagement: "Improved user engagement by 35%"
