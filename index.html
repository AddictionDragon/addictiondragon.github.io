About Me

Behavioral Data Scientist with a Ph.D. in Addiction Psychology and over 20 years of clinical experience. I essentially bridge the gap between deep human psychology and scalable technical analytics.

While many data scientists focus solely on the numbers, I translate clinical variables into actionable code. For example, I recently engineered a machine learning model to detect heart disease in undiagnosed patients—turning complex behavioral and health data into life-saving insights. My toolkit includes Python, SQL, and MLOps, and I’m experienced in deploying everything from Generative AI to AWS-backed production systems.

But what truly sets me apart is the endurance I bring to the work. I’m a former professional skater with Disney on Ice and a 7-time Ironman finisher. I apply that same 'go-the-distance' discipline to every data pipeline and project I manage. I’m looking to bring this blend of clinical depth and technical rigor to a team that’s using data to improve human outcomes.
