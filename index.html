About Me

Hello! I'm Addiction Dragon, a dedicated data science professional with a passion for uncovering patterns and insights from complex datasets. With expertise in statistical analysis, machine learning, and data visualization, I transform raw data into meaningful business solutions.

Beyond data science, I bring a unique background as a former professional ice skater with Disney on Ice and an accomplished endurance athlete. My journey includes multiple Ironman competitions, marathons, and a lifetime of dedication to excellence in both artistic and athletic pursuits.

I'm committed to continuous learning and staying at the forefront of data science innovations. My goal is to leverage data-driven decision making to create impactful solutions for real-world problems.
