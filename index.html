About Me

I am a licensed mental health professional with a Ph.D. in Addiction Psychology and over 15 years of experience in clinical assessment, psychotherapy, and behavioral health. With over 10 years of direct clinical experience providing mental health and substance use disorder treatment across diverse settings, I bring a comprehensive understanding of treatment delivery from both clinical and operational perspectives.

As a former CEO and Founder of a behavioral health organization, I have deep operational expertise in connecting clinical services to sustainable reimbursement models. My experience includes administration of state health plans for Blue Cross Blue Shield (BCBS), where I developed expertise in connecting clinical standards with utilization review processes to optimize third-party payer reviews, improve revenue cycle management, and ensure client-driven outcomes. My approach bridges clinical excellence with financial sustainability by developing policies aligned with CARF, Joint Commission standards, MCG guidelines, and URAC requirements.

Beyond clinical work, I bring doctoral-level expertise in quantitative research and data analysis, with published research in addiction psychology. I have served as an invited reviewer for APA journals, providing expert review in client-centered therapy, evidence-based treatment, and survivor guilt. I combine advanced statistical analysis skills with programming proficiency in Python, R, SQL, and HTML. Currently expanding my expertise through TripleTen's AI/ML bootcamp to integrate machine learning approaches into behavioral health research and practice.
